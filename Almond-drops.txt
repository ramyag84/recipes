Double boil the dark chocolate. Roast the almonds in a little bit of ghee. Take a tray and place a tracing paper on it. Spread some butter. Take 2 or 3 roasted almonds with a spoon and dip it in the chocolate syrup and drop it in the tray. Keep as many drops as you need and place the tray in the freezer for about 20 minutes. Delicious almond drops are ready.
